# Market Study — Hvac

## Executive Summary
The commercial HVAC services sector shows stable demand driven by recurring maintenance contracts and regulatory-driven retrofits. Multiples are compressed for very small deals but expand with scale and recurring revenue.

## EBITDA Multiples by Deal Size
- $2-<$5M: range 3.0-4.5x, median 3.8x, deals 24
- $5-<$10M: range 4.5-6.0x, median 5.2x, deals 37
- $10-<$15M: range 5.5-7.0x, median 6.1x, deals 15
- $15-<$25M: range 6.0-8.0x, median 6.8x, deals 9

## Value Drivers
- Percentage of recurring maintenance revenue
- Customer concentration (low concentration preferred)
- Service contract length and transferability
- Equipment ownership vs. leasing
- Geographic footprint and regional regulatory exposure
- Quality of installer teams and documented processes
