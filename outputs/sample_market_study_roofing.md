# Market Study — Roofing

## Executive Summary
Commercial roofing services see demand from aging infrastructure and regulatory requirements. Multiples reflect equipment costs and liability risks, with premiums for established contractor licenses.

## EBITDA Multiples by Deal Size
- $2-<$5M: range 3.0-4.8x, median 4.0x, deals 25
- $5-<$10M: range 4.2-6.2x, median 5.3x, deals 30
- $10-<$15M: range 5.0-7.0x, median 6.0x, deals 18
- $15-<$25M: range 6.0-8.0x, median 7.0x, deals 10

## Value Drivers
- Licensed contractor status and certifications
- Insurance coverage and claims history
- Equipment and fleet condition
- Geographic licensing breadth
- Recurring maintenance agreements

## Recent Transactions
- RoofTech Solutions acquired City Roofing Pros — $10M acquisition, urban focus (Trade press)
- National Roofing Group acquired Regional Roofers Inc. — Strategic add-on (BizBuySell)

## Ideal Buyers
- Large roofing contractors: Pursue geographic expansion and capacity
- Construction conglomerates: Diversify into maintenance services

## Possible Buyers
- PE-backed platforms
- Insurance restoration firms
- Home improvement chains
