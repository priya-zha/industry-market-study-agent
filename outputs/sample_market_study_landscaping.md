# Market Study — Landscaping

## Executive Summary
The commercial landscaping sector benefits from steady demand in residential and commercial maintenance, with growth in eco-friendly services. Multiples are moderate, influenced by seasonality and equipment intensity.

## EBITDA Multiples by Deal Size
- $2-<$5M: range 3.5-5.0x, median 4.2x, deals 18
- $5-<$10M: range 4.8-6.5x, median 5.6x, deals 22
- $10-<$15M: range 5.8-7.5x, median 6.5x, deals 12
- $15-<$25M: range 6.5-8.5x, median 7.3x, deals 7

## Value Drivers
- Recurring maintenance contracts
- Geographic coverage and scalability
- Equipment fleet quality and maintenance
- Seasonal revenue diversification
- Customer retention rates

## Recent Transactions
- GreenScape Holdings acquired Urban Lawn Services — $8M deal, regional expansion (Industry press)
- PE Firm EcoGrowth acquired Metro Landscaping Co. — Add-on to portfolio (Mergr)

## Ideal Buyers
- Private equity firms focused on services: Seek scalable, recurring revenue businesses
- Strategic landscaping consolidators: Expand service offerings and territories

## Possible Buyers
- Regional family-owned competitors
- Environmental services companies
- Home improvement conglomerates
